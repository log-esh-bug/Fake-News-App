About Us

We are Electronics Engineers and currently working on a project that detects the most accurate information that is spreading on the internet.

The aim of our project is to develop a system for detecting fake news using machine learning techniques. It involves preprocessing text data, training a model to classify news articles as real or fake, and deploying the model for real-time prediction.
